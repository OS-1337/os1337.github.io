a CLI-oriented minimal toybox/Linux + musl distribtion

Highly Compatible

  • Linux Kernel

  • Should be able to run on everything

Toybox Userland

  • Efficient, Sleek and Fast
  • Provides all the necessary basics

musl C-Library

  • Compact and Stable
  • Doesn't constantly brick Userspace unlike Glibc

Dropbear SSH Client

  • A sleek and efficient way to connect to your more powerful systems, ready to go.
  • "Make Terminals Great Again!"

Additional Tools

  • To make it a fully useable CLI-based Desktop OS
  • With Quality chosen above Quantity!

Easily Extensible

  • Just drop in your Linux binaries [compiled against musl] into the filesystem
  • Trivially expand your minimalist CLI desktop to your needs
